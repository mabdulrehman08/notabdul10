This is your video vault.

Anything inside `public/videos` is served directly by Next.js, so:

- `public/videos/reels/intro-loop.mp4` -> `/videos/reels/intro-loop.mp4`
- `public/videos/process/product-walkthrough.mp4` -> `/videos/process/product-walkthrough.mp4`
- `public/videos/archive/first-prototype.mp4` -> `/videos/archive/first-prototype.mp4`

Suggested structure:

- `reels/` for polished short clips you may feature on the site
- `process/` for demos, devlogs, experiments, and behind-the-scenes footage
- `archive/` for older versions you still want to keep around

Recommended formats:

- `.mp4` for widest browser support
- `.webm` if you want lighter looping background clips
